What sets it apart

What makes it different

Three things no other Odoo implementation tool does.

Starts with your business

Most Odoo setup tools assume you already know what to configure. This platform starts with the business question — what do you sell, how do you fulfil it, who approves what — and derives the configuration from those answers. If you skip a question, it activates the maximum possible scope so you never end up with a smaller implementation than your business requires.

Nothing runs without approval

Every configuration action is previewed before it runs and classified by safety level. You see the intended change, the target record or setting in Odoo, and the downstream consequences before you approve. Approval is mandatory — and the platform records a traceable log of every action executed and who authorised it.

Ready is not the same as done

The platform enforces the distinction between completing configuration and being ready to go live. Finishing a setup checklist does not mean your implementation is operationally ready. The platform tracks evidence at each critical step and requires explicit confirmation that the evidence is sufficient — because readiness is a separate determination from completion.

What it is not

Not a remediation tool Not a data repair utility No direct database access Does not replace your decisions

The platform does not fix broken or misconfigured Odoo installations. It supports only fresh implementations, controlled forward-safe expansions, and guided activation of unused modules — it does not work backwards through history. It also does not replace the business owner's role in making key decisions: it structures those decisions, explains their consequences, and records the outcome. Everything the platform does goes through the standard Odoo application layer.
